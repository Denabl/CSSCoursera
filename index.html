Wildlife in Costa Rica

In June of 2024, I traveled to Costa Rica for about 9 days. It was my first solo trip outside of a work trip, and I didn't speak the language!

Honestly, one of the most daunting parts of the trip was planning it, which I did at the last minute, with some much-needed help (thanks Kelly).

I met some wonderful people in the three different places I stayed (in 2 cities), and saw some staggeringly beautiful wildlife.
